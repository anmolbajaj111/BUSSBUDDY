Why Take a Weekend Getaway?

Benefits of exploring nearby destinations

Stress Relief

Short trips help reduce academic stress and refresh your mind for better focus.

Bonding Time

Great opportunity to bond with friends outside the campus environment.

Cultural Exposure

Experience local culture, food, and traditions beyond your university.

Student Travel Tips

Budgeting

  • Book bus tickets in advance for better prices
  • Look for student discounts at attractions
  • Share accommodation costs with friends
  • Try local street food for affordable meals

Packing

  • Pack light - just essentials for short trips
  • Carry a reusable water bottle
  • Bring power banks and chargers
  • Pack weather-appropriate clothing

Safety Advice

General Safety

  • Always travel in groups when possible
  • Keep emergency contacts handy
  • Inform someone about your travel plans
  • Be aware of your surroundings

Bus Travel

  • Note your bus number and conductor details
  • Keep valuables secure during travel
  • Arrive at the stop 10 minutes early
  • Save the bus company's contact number

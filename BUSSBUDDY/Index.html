Purpose
"We believe every student deserves safe, affordable, and accessible travel — and we’re on the road to make it happen."

BusBuddy was born out of a need to simplify everyday travel for students in and around LPU. With confusing routes, unreliable fare details, and no easy way to know how conductors behave, we wanted to create a platform that makes bus travel stress-free.
